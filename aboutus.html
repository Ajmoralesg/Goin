What do we do, and why do we do it?

We want to create meaningful social opportunities for students to make them more successful in life.

The future of education is hybrid. For Gen Z, hybrid is not new at all and hybrid friendships have been around for years. We were disappointed that in this hybrid world, there wasn't a good and safe way to find out who's goin' to the same university or even connect online with who is there too.

Many universities invested in taking education to a hybrid level. Next is the student experience! A student experience that deserves more hybrid fun, peer support, inclusion, and ethical data treatment.

At Goin' we improve the hybrid student experience by connecting your students the ethical way. A student-led way, effective in establishing meaningful hybrid friendships at your university to make students more successful in life.
